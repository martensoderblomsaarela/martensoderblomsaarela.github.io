Talk on Manchu Philology at the Qianlong Court, on YouTube

2022-08-12

On August 4, 2022 I gave a talk at Staatsbibliothek zu Berlin on the topic of "Manchu philology at the Qianlong court." The talk can now be viewed on YouTube. Unfortunately the questions asked during the Q&A are not very audible, and I failed to repeat them for the microphone.

The talk is based on two chapters from a book manuscript that I’m working on. More on that in a future post (hopefully).
